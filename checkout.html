ABOUT US

Renowned for supplying spectacular flowers throughout San Francisco and the Bay Area. Andrei and Lisa Krylov bring over fifteen years of experience to the Not Just Flowers Store and Design Studio. Not Just Flowers has built a strong reputation for consistently providing unique, perfectly tailored bouquets, utilizing the best seasonal fresh flowers and foliage. Have grown -up in England and Russia this husband and wife team have a strong European style, by mixing individual elements with a strong awareness of commercial and seasonal trends.
