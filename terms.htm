Privacy & Web Site Terms and Conditions of Use

At Not Just Flowers, your privacy is very important to us. Below you will find our privacy policy that offers you a better understanding of how we collect, use, communicate and disclose of your personal information:

  • Before or at the time of collecting personal information, we will identify the purposes for which information is being collected.

  • We will collect and use your personal information solely with the objective of fulfilling those purposes specified by us, unless we obtain the consent of the individual concerned or as required by law.

  • We will only retain personal information as long as necessary for the fulfillment of those purposes.

  • We will collect personal information by lawful and fair means and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned.

  • We will protect personal information by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.


Delivery Ordering Information
When your order is placed on the web site, you will see your online order confirmation number. You will receive an email confirmation once your order has been received. Same day delivery cutoff time is 2:00pm PST. Orders placed after 2pm will be delivered the following day.

Delivery Charges
Local delivery charges and CA sales tax of 8.5% are not included in the arrangement price and will be added to your total.

Seasonal Flowers and Substitutions
If we are out of a particular vase or type of flower, we may substitute a similar element of equal or greater value. The "look and feel" of our arrangements featured will remain the same, with our designers paying careful attention to overall shape, size, style, and color combinations. For example we will not substitute red roses for white orchids.

Delivery Time Requests
We will attempt to accommodate a delivery time within a 2-4 hour window per your request, however we can't guarantee an exact time of delivery. All deliveries will be completed prior to 7pm.

Incorrect Addresses
Incorrect addresses will result in a re-routing charge of $15.00 if our driver has already attempted delivery. The information we collect We ask for a phone number/ contact information of the recipient and customers only to help us make arrangements for delivery and for customer service purposes. We treat all of the information provided to us as confidential, and will never disclose or sell this information to any third party.
