ABOUT US

Not Just Flowers has built a strong reputation for consistently providing unique, perfectly tailored bouquets, utilizing the best seasonal fresh flowers and foliage. Have grown -up in England and Russia this husband and wife team have a strong European style, by mixing individual elements with a strong awareness of commercial and seasonal trends.
